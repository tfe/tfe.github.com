About Me

Hello, my name is Todd. I’ve been consulting for San Francisco tech companies for four years, helping them do more, move faster, or level up their teams. Maybe I can help yours?

How I Work

I generally do three types of engagements:

Team Augmentation: I integrate into an existing team to help them get more done, provide expertise, or just fill in until they can find a full-time hire. These are usually 3–5 days per week and last anywhere from a couple weeks to a several months.

On-Call Developer: Are you in marketing or customer service and tired of begging the engineering team for help with every website change you want to make? I run into this scenario all the time and I can help! We can set you up with a block of hours every month for all the odds-and-ends tasks that need to get done but you don’t want to burden your product team with.

Project Based: If you have a project you need developed, I can take it from designs and mockups to a market-ready product. Whether you don’t have a dev team or just want to free them up to work on other things, I can help. These engagements are usually a couple weeks’ worth of concentrated work and a couple extra days for follow-up and iteration.

Rates

I typically do bill for my time (“time and materials”) on a day rate of $2000 ($250/hour). I require a minimum of one day for any project.
